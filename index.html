About

I'm a third-year technical communication student at New Mexico Tech. I've lived my whole life in New Mexico -- long enough to understand its title, "The Land of Enchantment." Its culture and land instill in me a profound passion. Though my line of work does not find me on the range, I carry a love for the way of the cowboy with me, including a commitment to adaptability and hard work. A family legacy of ranching, banking and preaching has taught me that, whether sitting at a desk or in a saddle, a man should always do his best. I strive to bring small-town friendliness and good ol' southern gentlemanliness to every project.
